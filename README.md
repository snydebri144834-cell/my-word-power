## 🚀 素材实战演练 (Output Sandbox)

我们将这些词放入作文语境中，看看如何提升句子的“高级感”。

### 🏛️ 雅思写作 (社会议题：竞争与利己)
* **普通句子：** Some people only care about themselves in a competitive society.
* **升级表达：** In an increasingly competitive world, many individuals have evolved into **egoists** who prioritize personal gain over communal well-being.

### 🎓 英语一作文 (哲理性题目：助人与美德)
* **核心论点：** 真正的快乐往往源于对他人的奉献。
* **高级句式：** The essence of a harmonious society lies in **altruism**, where people find fulfillment in helping others rather than in purely selfish pursuits.
